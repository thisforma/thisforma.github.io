T R U E   C O S T

1

M O L D

The first step in the process involves making the clay from scratch, coloring it with the pigments and cutting it into the respective shapes.
Time: Half a day

2

D R Y

As our pieces are air-drying, they need to dry completely before moving on to the next step. This is the longest step in the entire process.
Time: 1 week

3

P E R F E C T

Unlike the previous one, this is the most demanding step of the entire job, as it involves sanding, drilling and varnishing the parts.
Time: 1 day

4

A S S E M B L Y

The assembly of the pieces is relatively simple, joining the rings and respective chains to the pieces.
Time: 15 minutes

5

P H O T O S

Photographs are essential for all product promotion, from photos with a model to photos of just the product. All photos are edited so as to be as uniform as possible.
Time: Half a day

6

M A R K E T I N G

With the photographs ready, the next step is to promote them on social networks and update our website. This is an ongoing work that is being renewed as we make new products.

7

P A C K A G I N G

The identity of a brand is present in the way it sends its products to the customer, and that is why we are the ones who print and make our packaging, with the exception of cardboard boxes.

8

V A T

This is the percentage of value we take back to us, based on work per part, amount of material used and time.

It is not possible for us to estimate the time for all the steps presented above, so the prices are estimated by approximation to the work that the task gives in general. If you have any questions, please don't hesitate to contact us!
