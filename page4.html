T H E   S T O R Y

If you are looking for a brand with sustainable accessories made in the most homemade way possible, you are in the right place!




Hi there!

My name is Beatriz and I'm a conservation biologist.
About 3 years ago I started my journey to reduce the amount of waste I used to do at home. As a biologist, nothing would make more sense to me than applying this rule to everything in my life.

Thisforma was born from a challenge to create a brand of unique and irreplicable accessories that would do justice to each person. I started by using polymer clay, but at a certain point it no longer made sense to use materials of harmful origin. So I had to find a natural alternative.

Thus was born the sustainable collection in which clay is handmade and pigments are natural ingredients that we use in our kitchen. It is a woman owned business where all the products are made sustainably by hand.

Why jewelry?  For its ability to increase our confidence and our well-being. Jewelry is not just an accessory, it is the empowerment of a more confident and self-assured being. And why not empower ourselves together in a more sustainable way?

The Founder,

Beatriz Afonso
