CONTRACT CELEBRATION 

To place an order for one or more products available on the website, you must respect the following procedures:
• Choose the product on the Thisforma website;
• Add the product to the “Cart”;
• Subsequently, click on “Paypal Checkout” or “Checkout”;
• Fill in the following mandatory fields: Name by nickname, Email and Shipping Address.
• Finally, you must click on “Pay Now”

Once the order is finalized, Thisforma will send you an “Order Confirmation” email. This email is a mere proof, which means that we have received your order. Always make sure to review your purchase before clicking on "Confirm Payment". Before clicking on "Confirm Payment", you have the possibility to edit all the information you have entered, such as the shipping or billing address, the payment information, or to add, change or withdraw products in the "Cart".

The receipt of the purchase will be sent to you by email.

PAYMENT

Payments can be made using Visa, Mastercard, Maestro or Paypal.
All orders are processed in the local currency of the respective country of purchase. In case your bank account is in another currency, the exchange rate used to debit your account will be automatically determined by Bank / Paypal and not by Thisforma.

- Visa, Mastercard, Maestro
After selecting the credit payment option, you must enter the necessary data for your credit card, namely the name of the cardholder, the type of credit card, the number, validity and security code of the card, and later , click on “Confirm Payment”. The charge on the card is made within a period that can vary up to 48 hours after the finalization of the order.

- Paypal
Paypal payment is a secure method used worldwide.
If payment is made via Paypal, the debit will be made after clicking on "Confirm Payment".
